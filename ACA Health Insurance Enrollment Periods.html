ACA Health Insurance Enrollment Periods

Know Your Deadlines!

Why Enrollment Periods Matter

Understanding the enrollment periods for ACA health insurance is crucial to ensure you apply on time and don't miss out on health coverage. The enrollment periods are set by the government and typically happen once a year.

Important Enrollment Deadlines

Open Enrollment Period

November 1, 2024 - December 30, 2024

Special Enrollment Period

Available for those who qualify due to special circumstances.

Medicaid Enrollment

Ongoing - Apply any time if you meet eligibility criteria.

What to Do Next

Make sure you're prepared to apply during the open enrollment period. You can check your eligibility, gather necessary documents, and decide which plan best suits your needs.
